PizzaNow! App — Stats That Sell

Your app isn't just a menu — it's a growth engine. Here's why pizza shops love it:

Event Promotions — Turn Local Happenings Into Sales
  • 20%-25% higher sales on event days (games, parades, festivals).
  • Local sports tie-ins boost foot traffic and online orders by up to 30%.
  • Community events increase new customer acquisition by 15%-20%.
  • Event push notifications open 25%-30% of the time, with 60% of customers acting within 2 hours.
  • Limited-time event specials convert 2x-3x better than ongoing discounts.
  • 65%-70% of customers feel stronger loyalty to restaurants active in their community.
Specials That Sell — Deals That Drive Orders Instantly
  • App-exclusive push notifications boost same-day sales by 15%-20%.
  • Limited-time offers (“Today Only”) convert 2x-3x better than regular discounts.
  • Customers redeeming specials spend 22% more per order, often adding sides and drinks.
  • Flash deals during slow hours can lift sales up to 25%.
  • Daily or weekly specials increase repeat visits by up to 20%.
  • 72% of customers are more loyal to restaurants with app-only specials.
Rewards Programs — Keep Customers Coming Back
  • Loyalty members order 2x more often than non-members.
  • Members spend 18%-30% more per ticket than non-members.
  • Rewards increase retention rates by at least 20%.
  • Push notifications about rewards have 4x-6x higher open rates than standard promos.
  • Time-sensitive rewards (“Double Points Today Only”) can lift same-day orders by up to 20%.
  • 73% of customers say they're more likely to recommend restaurants with rewards programs.
Online Fundraising — Raise Dough for Local Causes
  • Fundraiser days increase order volume by 15%-30%.
  • 25% of fundraiser orders come from first-time customers.
  • Online/app-based fundraisers have 2x-3x higher participation than paper or phone orders.
  • Push notifications for fundraisers generate 20%-25% open rates.
  • Average ticket size increases $5-$8 when orders support a cause.
  • Repeat orders increase 15%-20% after fundraiser participation.
  • 70% of participants report feeling more loyal to businesses that support their community.
The Bottom Line

With your app, you're not just selling pizza — you're:

  • Driving more orders, more often.
  • Boosting ticket size with irresistible deals.
  • Turning first-time buyers into loyal fans.
  • Becoming a community hero for local events and fundraisers.

PizzaNow! isn't just an app. It's your secret marketing engine that works while you bake.
