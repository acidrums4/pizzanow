Rewards Programs — Keep Them Coming Back with Your App

Free pizza. Bonus points. Cheesy incentives they can't resist.

When your customers know they're getting rewarded every time they order, they'll keep coming back — and your ovens will stay busy.

Turn First-Time Customers into Regulars

With your app's built-in rewards program, every order puts points in your customers' pockets.

They watch their points add up — and when they're close to a free pizza, they're not ordering from anyone else.

Instant Gratification

No punch cards to lose. No confusing rules.

Customers open your app, see their points balance, and know exactly how close they are to their next reward.

“You're just 15 points away from FREE Breadsticks!”

Deals That Feel Like Gifts

Your app lets you send surprise rewards to keep customers excited:

  • Birthday Freebies — a free dessert or breadsticks on their special day.
  • VIP Status — extra points for your top spenders.
  • Double Points Days — drive big spikes in orders during slow periods.
You Control the Rules

Set how points are earned, what rewards you offer, and when they're redeemed.

Run limited-time reward boosts to encourage bigger orders and repeat visits.

Track Loyalty Like a Pro

Your app's dashboard shows:

  • Who your top customers are.
  • How often they order.
  • Which rewards bring them back fastest.
The Bottom Line

With your app, rewards aren't just freebies — they're a proven way to keep customers loyal, increase order frequency, and make sure your name is the first one they think of when they're hungry.
