The Power of Online Fundraising – By the Numbers

Fundraisers Drive Orders and Awareness
  • Restaurants that partner with local schools, sports teams, or charities see 15-30% higher order volume on fundraiser days.
  • Fundraiser promotions attract new customers — 25% of orders come from first-time buyers during these events.
  • Local causes can expand your reach: 50% of fundraiser participants share promotions with friends and family.
Online & App-Based Fundraising Works Best
  • Online ordering fundraisers have 2–3x higher participation than paper-based or phone-based orders.
  • Push notifications about fundraisers generate an open rate of 20-25%, higher than standard marketing emails.
  • Customers complete orders 50% faster when they can sign up and order directly through an app.
Fundraisers Increase Average Order Value
  • Customers are more willing to add sides, drinks, or desserts when their purchase supports a cause — average ticket increases by $5-$8.
  • Fundraiser days can lift overall sales by 20% or more, especially when paired with limited-time deals.
Loyalty & Community Goodwill
  • Restaurants that run local fundraisers see repeat orders from participants increase by 15–20% in the following month.
  • 70% of participants report feeling more loyal to businesses that support their community.
